英语六级

议论文模板

开头端

  • owing to the pervasive proliferation of xxx,xxx has intrigued the public.

  • As far as I am concerned/from my prespective,the merits/demerits of xxx are innumerable and manifold.

  • I am in line with the view that ……（我与……观点一致）

分论点

分论点1

  • 主旨句：
    • First and foremost/ in the first place
    • what must be prioritized is that,……
  • 道理论证：
    • There are myriad reasons for this.
    • The primary/chief cause is that……
    • The second noteworthy reason is that ……
    • As is known to all,……
  • 举例论证：
    • xxx is a case in point.
    • This point is best illustrated by the case of xxx
  • 小结：
    • Therefore,it reinforces my point that ……
    • It is xxx that plays a pivotal role in ……

分论点2

  • 主旨句：
    • Furthermore/In addition/In the second place
    • never can people ignore the indispensability of xxx.

结尾段

  • To sum up/In a nutshell/All in all
  • It is natural/reasonable to reach the conclusion that,……
  • Taking into account all the factors disscuss above.（考虑到上述所有因素）

注意

写到黄金分割点处。

凑字数

  • because
    • There is a perfecty reasonable explanation for this.
  • For example
    • A real-life experience that provides a vivid example is this:
  • we should
    • It is imperative for us to take measures to do ……
  • can help
    • be instrumental in
  • can make
    • enable sb. to do
  • only in this way can we fulfill success and only in this way can our society become more harmonious to live in.

图表作文

描述图表

  • The above chart/graph explicitly illustrates the percentage/number/amount of …… from …… to ……

  • <趋势> As is evidently shown/reflected by the figure in the chart,……

  • 题干核心词 has been on rise/decrease/drops + steeply/steadily/dramatic from …… to ……

  • 结论 From the considerable/moderate decline/rise in the chart,结论

  • Simple as it seems,what the picture tries to reveal is thought-provoking.

解释原因

  • There are myriad reasons accounting for this.
  • The prime/chief cause is that ……
  • The second noteworthy reason is that ……
  • 《凑》Opponents may also cite a host of seeming correct reasons.Nonetheless,the aforementioned reasons are the most convincing.（尽管如此）

凑字数 + 展望未来

  • To sum up/In a nutshell/All in all
  • 《凑》considering all the factors disscuss above.
  • It is natural/reasonable to reach the conclusion that……,there is a growing/decreasing tendency for sb. to do sth.
  • 《展望未来》 and utmost importance shoule be attached to this trend/phenonmenon.

猛增：boom,surge,skyrocket

势头正猛：is gaining momentum
